Israeli troops killed four Palestinians Tuesday as Israeli forces blockaded Palestinian towns and villages in reprisal for the killing of four Israelis.
Two of the Palestinians ' deaths were in the Gaza Strip, the third was in the West Bank town of Ramallah.
Late Tuesday, Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak met with UN Secretary General Kofi Annan in London during a stopover at Heathrow airport on his way home from a visit in the United States.
