UN Secretary General Kofi Annan has nominated former Dutch Prime Minister Ruud Lubbers as the new UN High Commissioner for Refugees.
Mr. Annan praised the record of the 61-year-old Mr. Lubbers as a government leader, academic and private citizen, saying this made him the right man for the job.
If the General Assembly approves the nomination, Mr. Lubbers will replace Sadako Ogata of Japan on January 1st.
