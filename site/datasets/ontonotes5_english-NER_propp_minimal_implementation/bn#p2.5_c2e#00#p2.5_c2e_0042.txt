In his speech, Wen Jiabao conducted a concrete mapping-out of primary objectives, tasks and important issues related to economic work next year.
It was pointed out at the meeting that this year is the outset of the Eleventh Five Year Plan period.
We will hold high the great banner of Deng Xiaoping Theory and the important thought of the Three Represents, comprehensively implement the concept of scientific development and conscientiously carry out the Eleventh Five Year Plan.
New achievements have been made in socialist economic construction, political construction, cultural construction, social construction and party construction.
Modernization construction in national defense and the military will continue to be pushed forward.
In response to new situations and new movements in the economic and social development of our country and outstanding contradictions in the operation of the economy, we will continue to strengthen and improve macro control and promote reform and opening up.
We will further improve conditions for development ; we will further optimize development environment ; and we will make further development momentum better and better.
The relatively fast growth showing in the national economy, better economic efficiency and relatively low price levels constitute an excellent outset for the Eleventh Five Year Plan period.
Through the joint efforts of the entire party and the people of all nationalities of the nation, our country's economic power and comprehensive national strength have continued to increase, its international influence has continued to grow and the lives of the people have steadily improved.
We have again taken a solid step toward the target of national construction of a middle-class society.
