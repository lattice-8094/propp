The International Football Federation will pick its player of the year and the Special Player of the Century award that pits Argentine hero Diego Maradona against Brazilian legend Pele.

Also nominated, Revaldo of Brazil and French hero Zenedine Zidane.
Zidane led France to the European title this year after helping France win the World Cup title two years ago.
There are 27 nominees for the Player of the Century award, but Maradona and Pele are the favorites.
Pele led Brazil to three World Cup titles and scored more than 1200 goals.
