Al Gore's favorability rating is slipping, and for the first time since he emerged as Bill Clinton's running mate in 1992, a majority of American adults now view him unfavorably.
The latest Gallup Poll, completed Monday, found 52 % of those questioned have an unfavorable view of Gore, compared to 39 % before the election.
46 % have a favorable view of Gore, compared to 56 % before the election.
