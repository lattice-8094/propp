On Saturday evening, the Jordanian Hashemite Charity Association sent humanitarian aid convoy number 220 to the Palestinian Territories, in coordination with the Crisis Management Center in the General Command of the Armed Forces.
The Association Secretary General, Abd Al Salam Al Abadi, said that the convoy consisted of ten trucks : one from Tekiat Um Ali, one from the Jordan River Organization, one from the Royal Jordanian, four trucks from a Turkish charity association, one carrying medicine from the Jordanian Pharmacists ' Union, and two trucks from prominent Jordanian citizens.
He added that the trucks carried 108 tons of various food supplies, like flour, baby formula, canned food, powdered milk, nutrition packages, tea, and medicine.
He noted that Jordan remains a gate of goodness and a bridge of giving through which humanitarian aid passes to the Palestinian people, who are suffering siege and hunger at the hands of the occupation forces.
The association sent convoy number 220, one of the Hashemite aid convoys, to the Palestinian people.
He asserted that it will continue to send aid to the Palestinian people.
The convoy is expected to cross King Hussein Bridge towards the Palestinian Territories tomorrow morning, Monday, at which time four trucks will head to the West Bank and six will head to the Gaza Strip.
