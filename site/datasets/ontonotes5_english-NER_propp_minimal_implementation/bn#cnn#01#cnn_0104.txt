United Nations health officials say the infection rate for new AIDS cases has remained constant, but the death rate is up.
According to the World Health Organization, 36 million people worldwide are now infected with HIV, the virus that causes AIDS.
The organization estimates there will be 5.3 million new cases this year.
An estimated 3 million people will die from AIDS this year.
That's expected to bring the total deaths worldwide to nearly 22 million people.
