Directors elected R. Marvin Womack, currently vice presidentproduct supply, purchasing, to head the company's Washington, D.C., office.
As vice presidentnational-government relations, Mr. Womack will work with P&G's top management and with the company's government-relations staff `` to represent P&G's interests at the federal level, '' said John G. Smale, chairman and chief executive officer.
Mr. Smale said the appointment `` recognizes the growing influence of government on our business. ''
Mr. Womack, 53 years old, has been with the big producer of household products, food and pharmaceuticals for 30 years.
